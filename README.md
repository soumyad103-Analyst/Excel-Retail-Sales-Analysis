# Excel-Retail-Sales-Analysis
# Busines Problem 
A retail company wants to understand its sales performance across regions, product categories, and time to improve profitability and operational decisions.
## Dataset Overview
Personal data, product data, date and time data, calculated columns

## KPIs Designed
Total sales, Total profit, profit margin %, Average sales(Region), Average order value
# Analysis Approach
Cleaned the dataset, made new columns
Added Pivot tables and charts connected to slicers

# Key Insights
Regionally, profit remains stable, But West region underperforms in both Sales and Profit.
West-Furniture has weak performance with just 54.07 lakh Total sales. Since, furniture's average sale is 65.21 lakh and in west its less than that, its due to low demand
Accessories contribute lowest to Total sales and Profit but has decent Profit margin (32.00%),suggesting less demand even after correct pricing.

# Recommendations
Promoting furniture products in west and Accessories | Total sales Increase, Total Profit Increase | High | Ops Head
Restructure pricing and discount | Total Sales Increase, Total Profit Increase | High | Finance Head
Manage Inventory during seasonal High demand | Total Sales Increase | Medium | Ops Head
